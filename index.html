Accounts

If you've commenced trading on the forex markets, you must determine which account type best matches your skill level, knowledge, and experience. At Forexx BROKERS, we offer the following types of accounts:

Micro Account
A micro account is intended mainly for retail investors who desire exposure to foreign exchange (forex) trading but cannot compromise substantial capital. The minimum contract size for a micro account, also known as a micro lot, is 1,000 units of currency, or a hundredth of a standard lot. Micro accounts provide higher leverage up to 1:3000 and fixed spreads beginning at three pips.

This kind of account also comes commission-free, with a maximum of 200 open positions and pending orders, the same as a Cent account. The minimum volume a trader can execute is one micro lot, whereas the maximum volume will typically vary based on the account's equity. To establish a Micro account, a $5 deposit is necessary.
Standard Account
Standard accounts are the most prevalent and adaptable account categories. The Standard Account is ideal for traders seeking a conventional FX trading experience, as it provides extremely competitive spreads and no commissions. The cost to you is the spread.

This account gives the individual access to the $100,000 value of standard currency units. A larger deposit of $100 is necessary to establish a Standard account at FBS. However, its spread is variable and begins at 0.5 pip. Again, there is no commission, and the maximum leverage is 1:3000.
ECN Account
ECN (Electronic Communication Network) accounts are valued by traders who seek the most advantageous trading conditions. The ECN account enables direct transactions between customers and vendors. For the creation of an ECN account, an initial deposit of $1,000 is necessary. It is expensive but well worth it! ECN accounts offer the quickest market execution, the lowest spreads (from -1 pip), the finest quotations with no delay, and access to an extensive range of liquidity providers.

Say 'goodbye' to all trading limitations and enjoy many open positions and pending orders. In addition, there is no limit on the number of orders, and all kinds of trading strategies are permissible.
VIP Account
VIP accounts are for high-volume merchants seeking competitive pricing and additional benefits. The 'VIP' account needs a minimum deposit of $20,000, is commission and swap-free, and requires no minimum deposit. With this account, consumers can trade on 100 percent raw spreads. This account category includes three complimentary consultations with a chief market analyst.
